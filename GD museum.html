Gedee Museum

Gedee Car Museum or GD Museum, is a private vintage car museum located in the city of Coimbatore, India.

The museum was founded by GD Gopal in 2015 as a commemoration of his late father, Gopalswamy Doraiswamy Naidu.[1] During its inauguration, the museum was endowed with a distinctive assortment of automobiles originating from Britain, Germany, Japan, France, Spain, and the United States

1.1947 Austin Sheerline A125 2.1980 Oldsmobile Delta 88
