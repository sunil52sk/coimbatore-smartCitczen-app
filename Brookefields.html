Brookefields Mall

Brookefields is a shopping mall located on Brookebond Road (Krishnasamy Road) in Coimbatore, India. It was opened in May 2009. The mall has outlets from major clothing and apparel brands, a 06-screens multiplex cinema, and a food court serving multi-cuisine dishes. History.

The mall has so many facilities. A banquet hall was opened lately the previous year. There is a food court located on the third floor with a variety of foods and popular brands such as KFC, Pizza Hut, McDonald's, and much more. The PVR Cinemas, is 6-screened on the fourth floor.
